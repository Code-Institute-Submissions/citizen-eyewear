Meet the team


Samia Jansen is our resident optometrist and manager. She has managed Citizen Eyecare since taking over the business over 10 years ago, and has a wealth of knowledge about eyecare and styling. Samia loves her job and she always has a smile on her face! She has additional qualifications in paediatric testing.
Samia owns six chickens and two dogs and loves going on hikes with her husband and three children.



David Watkins is a recently qualified dispensing optician who has worked with us since he started his training in 2017. He has an eye for style and can help you find the frames that are best suited to your face shape, skin colour, or even to compliment your hair colour.
In his spare time, David is a rally driver and has competed in over 300 races!



Aarav Ram is our part-time optometrist. He has worked with us for 15 years and has extensive experience in both high-street opticians and hospital settings. What Aarav can't tell you about contact lenses, nobody can!
Aarav is a keen artist, and loves both painting and sculpture - in fact, some of his art is on display in the shop.



Erin O'Connor is our marvellous receptionist who joined the team in 2020. She brings with her an eye for style, having previously worked in the fashion industry, and is more than happy to aid you with choosing a new frame for your glasses, as well as manning the phones and arranging appointments and deliveries.
Erin has a black belt in three martial arts and teaches at a local judo club.
