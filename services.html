Our services

We offer both private and NHS eye examinations (if you are eligible). If you are unsure which eye test would suit you best, please contact us using the form and one of the team will be in touch!


Service Description Price
Eye Exam with Retinal Photography General check up of the health of your eyes, includes retinal photography £40
Eye Exam with OCT Scan Includes an eye exam and check up, retinal photography, and state-of-the-art OCT photography to map the back of your eye and discover any issues early £70
Contact Lenses (First Appointment) An appointment with an optometrist to test to see if you are an ideal candidate for contact lenses and discuss options £30
Contact Lens 1 Year Review A checkup to assess suitability of contact lenses and discuss options (this is required yearly to continue ordering contact lenses) £20
Eyewear Styling Consultation A consultation with our optician to discuss the ideal frame to suit your style and face shape - with a fully qualified eyewear stylist £50
